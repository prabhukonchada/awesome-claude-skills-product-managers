# Frequently Asked Questions


## How does HydraSync track water intake?

A sensor in the bottle cap detects when water is dispensed. The sensor is sophisticated enough to distinguish between sips and pours, measuring volume accurately to within 1% tolerance. This data is transmitted to the companion mobile app via Bluetooth or WiFi and automatically synced to your health apps.

## What health apps does HydraSync integrate with?

HydraSync currently syncs with Apple Health, Google Fit, Fitbit, and Strava. Additional integrations with popular wellness platforms are in development based on user feedback.

## How do the LED reminders work?

The bottle's LED indicator pulses gently with increasing frequency when you fall behind your daily hydration goal. The pulsing intensity adjusts based on your personal patterns, so it's noticeable enough to catch your attention but not jarring or disruptive in a professional setting.

## How long does the battery last?

A single charge lasts approximately 7 days of normal use. The bottle uses low-power Bluetooth and an efficient LED system to maximize battery life. Charging is done via a magnetic USB-C connector on the base of the bottle.

## What if I refill my water bottle with filtered or tap water?

HydraSync tracks water intake regardless of the type of water. The sensor detects liquid movement, so tap water, filtered water, sparkling water, and other beverages are all counted toward your hydration goal. Only the sensor tip touches water, and it's made from medical-grade materials that resist corrosion and contamination.

## Is HydraSync waterproof?

Yes. The bottle carries an IP67 water-resistance rating, meaning it can withstand submersion up to 1 meter for 30 minutes. The cap and sensor are fully sealed, and the electronics are housed in a waterproof chamber separate from the drinking area.

## How much does HydraSync cost?

HydraSync is priced at $49. This includes the smart bottle, the sensor-equipped cap, a USB-C charging cable, and lifetime access to the companion app. There are no subscription fees or ongoing costs.

## When will HydraSync be available?

Pre-orders are open starting April 5, 2026. Initial shipments are expected to begin in May 2026. Orders will be fulfilled on a first-come, first-served basis.

## Who is HydraSync designed for?

HydraSync is built for health-conscious professionals aged 25–45 who understand the importance of hydration but struggle to drink enough water during busy workdays. It's ideal for anyone using health tracking apps like Apple Health, Fitbit, or Strava who wants an automated way to monitor hydration as part of their overall wellness routine.

## Is there a warranty?

Yes. Every HydraSync bottle comes with a 2-year limited warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. Accidental damage and normal wear are not covered, but our customer support team can discuss affordable repair or replacement options.

## Can I use HydraSync with my Apple Watch or Fitbit?

HydraSync syncs data to your Apple Health or Fitbit account, so hydration information appears on your devices automatically. We're also exploring native Apple Watch and Fitbit app experiences for future releases, which would allow you to see hydration goals and reminders directly on your wearables.

## How accurate is the sensor?

The sensor is calibrated to within 1% accuracy, matching the precision of laboratory-grade measurement equipment. However, accuracy can be affected by water temperature extremes (below 5°C or above 40°C) or very slow sips, which may take longer to register. In typical use, most users will find the tracking to be consistently reliable.
